Tiger


Tiger cubs stay with their mom for about two years, then they become independent and create their own groups.

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris
  • Type: Mammal
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Life Span: 8-10 Years
  • Size: Body is 5-6 feet & Tail is 2-3 feet
  • Weight: 240 to 500 pounds

Tigers can be found in forest habitats. Tigers have strong, muscular bodies. Tigers love to swim, especially to keep cool during hot days. They are one of the few striped cat species. Their tail is about half the length of their bodies. Tigers can be orange, white, golden and almost snow white.
