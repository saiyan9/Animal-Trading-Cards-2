Whale Shark

The whale shark is the largest fish, even though the maximum size is unknown.

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Rhincodon Typus
  • Type: Fish
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Life Span: 70 Years
  • Size: 18 to 32.8 feet
  • Weight: Up to 21 tons

Whale sharks are filter feeders. Their favorite food is plankton. Whale sharks prefer warm water. They populate the tropical seas. During spring, they migrate to the central west coast of Australia. This area has an abundant supply of plankton.
